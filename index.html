Robotics is a branch of engineering and computer science that involves the conception, design, manufacture and operation of robots. The objective of the robotics field is to create intelligent machines that can assist humans in a variety of ways Hanna, K. (2023) The different types of Robots are mainly inspired from nature these robots are called biomimicry robot.Spiders are one of the most fascinating species found in nature and they stand out for their agility, adaptability, and diversity in navigating different environments taking the inspiration our project the design is to build a spider robot which can go in all the terrains it can be used for diffrent scenarios like surveillance , tracking people or an object this project have lots of scope in diffrent sectors using cutting edge technologies like 3d printing and Computer Aided Design (CAD) we also used 12 Fs90mini 9g servo motors to make the robot move with stability where it has a rotation of 120 degrees and it just need and small amout of energy which will be supplied through Lithium-ion batteries.
