CONCLUSION

After successfully developing and creating the spider robot with 3D printing and servo motors to move the joints and limbs, we can say that the project met its goals. The spider robot can move its joints and make numerous actions, proving the effectiveness of the design, as well as the possibilities of 3D printing and servo motor technology:
