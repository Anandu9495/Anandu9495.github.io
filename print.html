PROTOTYPING WITH 3D PRINTING

We used Blender 3D printing to prototype the spider robot with servo motors. It has a number of advantages. One of the primary benefits is the opportunity to test and refine our design quickly, which lets to enhance the functionality and performance of the robot. Complex geometries and unique items that would be difficult or impossible to fabricate using conventional manufacturing methods can be created with 3D printing.
